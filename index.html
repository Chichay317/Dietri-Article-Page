TIPS AND EXERCISE TO ACHIEVE YOUR BODY GOALS

This year, things have changed in your life. New rules have been set and new traits have been adopted. But, has there been a change in terms of fitness and health? Most people set healthy goals each year and abandon them, but, when it comes to other aspects of life, they try to follow up. You need to be able to make plans for your health the same way you do for your finances. Everyone wants to get that 'summer body' but is not ready to put in the work required. Do not worry, getting your body goals is easier than you think. You need to be committed and ready to take control of your life.

Here are some tips that can help you on this journey;

(a) Set realistic and specific goals: You need to be clear about what you want on your health and fitness journey. Lay out specific steps you intend to take to get your desired body. Do not set goals like 'I want to lose weight'. Instead set something like 'I want to lose 10kg by August'. This promotes accountability.

(b) Stop Procrastinating: The best time to begin your journey is now. Take baby steps. No matter how little, with time and consistency, all your work will pay off.

(c) Drinking water: Drinking adequate water a day helps in weight loss and has a lots of benefits.

(d) Getting the right amount of sleep: The reason you don't feel the zeal to get up and work out could be because you're not getting enough sleep.

(e) Incorporate Cardio into your daily routine: Cardio increases your heart rate thereby burning that excess fat.

(f) Try Strength Training: Strength training is the use of your body weight or equipment to build strength, muscle mass and endurance. This boosts your body's metabolism and aids in the burning of those extra calories.

(g) Try HIIT: HIIT means High-intensity Interval Training. It is short bursts of intense exercises alternated with low-intensity recovery periods. It can be done within 10 minutes.

(h) Have a Healthy Meal Plan.

Food plays the most important role when it comes to achieving your goals. 'You are what you eat', It means the type of food you ingest determines what you look like. A high percentage of your health and fitness problems can be solved by eating the right food in the right amounts. According to the 80/20 rule, 80% focus should be placed on nutrition and 20% on exercise to lose that weight.
